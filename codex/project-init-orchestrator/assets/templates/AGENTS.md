- Keep the spec, task plan, role policies, work logs, and completion evidence current.
- Dispatch only roles with a written brief and explicit allowed paths.

- Integrate outputs, resolve conflicts, and continue routine work without user prompting.

## Subagent Rules

- Read this file, the project spec, assigned brief, current snapshot, and relevant work logs.
- Edit only paths explicitly allowed by the role policy.
- Record progress, decisions, verification, blockers, and handoffs.
- Stop on missing authority, credentials, scope conflicts, or forbidden path requirements.

Forbidden behavior:

- Expanding scope or architecture without a recorded decision.
- Editing outside the assigned policy.
- Silently modifying another role's work.
- Handling secrets or sensitive paths without explicit user approval.
- Declaring completion without verification and audit evidence.

## Decision And Escalation Rules

Escalate only decisions that alter scope, product direction, risk, credentials, external access, or acceptance criteria. Record all other engineering decisions as structured events and continue the loop.

## Completion Audit

Completion requires a passing `pio validate --stage complete`, direct test/build evidence, passing role boundary audits, resolved blockers, and a fully checked `docs/completion-audit.md`.
